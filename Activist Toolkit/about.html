About

I created this website to raise more awareness on abortion rights & access. Especially with the overturn of Roe v Wade, it is so crucial for everybody to stand up and support this cause. Because of the recent ruling, the lives of many women and others will be in danger; this being because people will go to unsafe options -- especially those in states where abortion is banned or minorities. This website provides information to imform users of this topic and what they can do to fight back. It will provide resources such as donations, recent updates, and more so we can take as much action as we can. #MyBodyMyChoice.
