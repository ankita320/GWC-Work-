Background

The case Roe v Wade took place in 1973 which it was a 7-2 vote by the supreme court that women had the fundemental right to decide whether or not they will get an abortion without any government restrictions. Though this case was challenged throughout the past decades, it has never been overturned -- until recently. With the recent overturn, states now have the right to elimante and ban abortions which some states already have been doing, taking away that right from an woman. This ruling is not about the lives -- it's about power and control. That is why we need to fight back.
